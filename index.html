What I'm all about...

Before getting into web development full time, I created seasonal drinks for Intelligentsia Coffee by day and performed a range of comedy shows by night. I've also been lucky enough to work on a handful of film projects and pull on those skills for project management.

My approach to web development is heavily influenced by my background in performance. Attention to detail reflects in the back end coding while creativity and adaptability shine in the front end. From the inception of an idea to the execution, I'll be ready to hop on board.
